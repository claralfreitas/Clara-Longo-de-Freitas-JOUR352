The Howard Center of Investigative Journalism is a multidisciplinary program at Phillip Merrill College of Journalism that allows students to work with news organizations in investigative projects.

I started to work with the center Jan. of 2020 as a reporter for a national project that partnered with NPR. In my first semester with the center, I researched and interpreted local, municipal and state laws and court records, and interviewed city officials and relevant sources. I also worked on a long-form stand-alone story for the project.
